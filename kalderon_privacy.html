Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025 · Compliant with Israeli Privacy Protection Law (PPL) 2024

1. Who We Are

AKalderon Arts (A&L Kalderon) is the estate and licensing body for the works of Israeli graphic designer and artist Asher Kalderon (1929–2018). Our website at akalderon-arts.com presents his artistic legacy and enables licensing inquiries.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only information you voluntarily provide:

  • Name, email address and phone number when you contact us or submit a licensing inquiry
  • Messages and correspondence you send us

We do not collect browsing data, location data, or personal profiles. We do not use tracking pixels or behavioral advertising tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and licensing requests
  • To send information about available works and editions you have requested
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

This website uses essential cookies only — required for basic site functionality (e.g., remembering your cookie preferences). We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking cookies without your explicit consent.

You may withdraw cookie consent at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer. Essential cookies only will remain active.

5. Data Retention

Contact information is retained for up to 3 years from the date of last correspondence, after which it is securely deleted. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights (PPL 2024)

Under the Israeli Privacy Protection Law 2024 you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction.
